About Me

“I turn data into direction—and insights into action.”

I'm Yunita Sulistiyo, a data enthusiast with a background in Informatics Engineering and a strong drive to uncover the stories behind the numbers. Through real-world experiences in programs like MSIB by Kampus Merdeka and internships, I've honed my skills in data analysis, business intelligence, and digital product development.

I'm comfortable using tools like SQL, Python, BigQuery, and Looker Studio to create reports and dashboards that are easy to read and useful for decision-making. I've also worked on projects in marketing, mental health, and game sales, turning complex information into simple and clear insights.
